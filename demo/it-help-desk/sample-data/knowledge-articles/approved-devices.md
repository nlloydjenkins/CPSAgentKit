# Approved Devices and BYOD Policy

## Company-Issued Devices

All Contoso employees receive a company-issued laptop. The standard models are:

| Role          | Device             | Specs                     |
| ------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------- |
| General staff | Dell Latitude 5540 | i5, 16 GB RAM, 256 GB SSD |
| Developers    | Dell Latitude 7440 | i7, 32 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD |
| Design team   | MacBook Pro 14" M3 | M3, 18 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD |
| Executives    | Dell Latitude 9440 | i7, 32 GB RAM, 1 TB SSD   |

### Requesting a Replacement

If your laptop is damaged, underperforming, or more than 3 years old:

1. Contact IT Support through the IT Help Desk agent in Teams
2. Describe the issue (damage, performance, age)
3. IT will assess whether a repair, upgrade, or replacement is appropriate
4. Typical turnaround: 3-5 business days for a replacement

### Returning a Device

When leaving Contoso or receiving a replacement:

1. Back up any personal files you need (IT will wipe the device)
2. Return the device to your local IT point person:
   - **London:** IT Desk, Floor 2
   - **Manchester:** IT Desk, Floor 3
   - **Edinburgh:** Reception, Floor 1
3. You will receive a confirmation email once the device is processed

## Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)

Contoso allows personal devices for specific purposes under the BYOD policy.

### What's Allowed

| Use Case                            | Allowed Devices      | Requirements                              |
| ----------------------------------- | -------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Email and calendar (Outlook mobile) | iOS 15+, Android 12+ | Microsoft Intune Company Portal installed |
| Teams (messaging and calls)         | iOS 15+, Android 12+ | Microsoft Intune Company Portal installed |
| SharePoint/OneDrive (view only)     | iOS 15+, Android 12+ | Microsoft Intune Company Portal installed |

### What's NOT Allowed

- Accessing Dataverse, CRM, or finance systems from personal devices
- Storing company documents locally on personal devices (use OneDrive instead)
- Connecting personal devices to the **Contoso-Corp** Wi-Fi network (use **Contoso-Guest** instead)
- Using personal devices as your primary work device

### Setting Up a Personal Device

1. Install the **Microsoft Intune Company Portal** app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Open Company Portal and sign in with your Contoso email
3. Follow the enrollment prompts — this allows Contoso to manage work apps on your device without accessing personal data
4. Once enrolled, install Outlook and Teams from the Company Portal
5. Sign in to Outlook and Teams with your Contoso account

### Security on Personal Devices

Once enrolled in Intune, the following policies apply automatically:

- Device must have a screen lock (PIN, fingerprint, or face recognition)
- Device must not be jailbroken or rooted
- Work data is encrypted and contained in a managed partition
- IT can remotely wipe **work data only** if the device is lost or you leave Contoso — personal photos, apps, and data are not affected

## Monitors, Keyboards, and Peripherals

Standard peripherals are provided at each desk:

- 24" Dell monitor
- Dell keyboard and mouse

If you need additional peripherals (second monitor, ergonomic keyboard, headset), submit a request through IT Support with a brief justification.
